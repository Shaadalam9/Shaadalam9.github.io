#### Introduction
Automotive Societal Factors addresses the relation between a car or other vehicle, its human driver, and its dynamic environment. The course focuses on technological, social, and legal perspectives of autonomous driving, including user perception, ethics, safety, data security, and future mobility.

#### My role
- 2024: lecturer.

#### Learning topics
- Human centred design and evaluation in automotive contexts.
- Societal dimensions of autonomous driving.
- Legal and ethical issues in future mobility.
- User perception and safety in automated vehicles.

#### Teaching format
The course combines lectures with group based assignments in which students examine real world questions around autonomous mobility and its broader societal impact.
